About UNEC

UNEC (Azerbaijan State University of Economics) is among the top universities of the region with over 20,000 students and a wide range of international programs. Our mission is to create future leaders in business, technology, and economics while providing a world-class educational environment.

Economics

Learn economics, finance, and global market strategies with leading professors and international research opportunities.

Develop leadership, communication, and entrepreneurial skills with practical projects and real-world business cases.
